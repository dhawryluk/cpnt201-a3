"Welcome to Derek Hawryluk's Digital Oasis: Where Web Development Meets Creativity and Adventure"

Welcome to the digital realm of Derek Hawryluk, a budding web developer with a passion for crafting digital experiences that resonate. As you step into his virtual space, you're greeted with a fusion of creativity, technical prowess, and a dash of personal flair.


Derek's website serves as a gateway to his world of web design excellence. Specializing in web design, wireframing, and responsiveness, he leverages his strong foundation in coding fundamentals to bring visions to life. Each project is a testament to his dedication to creating user-centric designs that leave a lasting impression.


Beyond the screen, Derek's interests and hobbies add depth to his persona. A connoisseur of both coffee and beer, he finds inspiration in the simple pleasures of life, which often infuse his work with a sense of warmth and familiarity. When he's not immersed in the digital realm, you'll likely find him exploring the great outdoors, camera in hand, capturing moments of beauty and serenity.


Photography isn't just a pastime for Derek—it's a passion that complements his creative journey. Through his lens, he captures the essence of nature, human connection, and the world around him, inviting viewers to see the world through his eyes.


Derek Hawryluk's website isn't just a portfolio—it's a reflection of his identity, his values, and his commitment to excellence. Whether you're in need of a captivating website, a memorable user experience, or simply seeking inspiration, Derek invites you to embark on a digital journey where innovation meets artistry.
